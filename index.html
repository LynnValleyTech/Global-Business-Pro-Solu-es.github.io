About Global Business Pro

Founded in Vancouver, BC, Global Business Pro has been helping organizations navigate complex business challenges for over 15 years. Our team of experienced consultants brings diverse industry expertise and a practical approach to problem-solving.

What sets us apart is our commitment to delivering measurable results while building the internal capabilities of our clients. We don't just provide recommendations—we work alongside you to implement solutions that drive sustainable improvement.

Our Vancouver roots give us unique insight into the local business landscape, while our global perspective allows us to bring international best practices to our clients.
